WHAT IS UNPLANNED PILLAR?

  • Unplanned Pillar caters for all the Client Escalations which are raised in customer portal either by clients or CD/CSMs/Support teams on behalf of clients.

  • Client Escalation backlog is prioritized and actioned based upon Client impact assessment (Escalation, Business Impact, Delivery Required, Commercial Impact) which is determined by Client facing teams.

  • R&D teams review and deliver fixes basis prioritization (Scoring). Different phases in the delivery cycles are updated by updating the status of the corresponding JIRA Ticket (Clex) which in turn gets reflected in Client Ticket ( Customer Portal).This is an end to end automated process.
